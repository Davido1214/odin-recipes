Quesadillas


Description


Quick and easy snack, Quesadillas are made by pan frying a tortilla and once it starts to develop a color fill half of it with Queso blanco or mozzarella and fold the tortilla over itself then let the cheese melt inside and for the tortilla to cook until beautifully gold and crisp in which it'll ready!


Ingredients

  • 1 Tortilla
  • 1/3 cup of cheese of your choice

Steps

  1. Place tortilla on hot pan
  2. Once the bottom begins to develop color flip
  3. Add Cheese to the tortilla and fold it over on itself
  4. let the outside of the tortilla develop color and become crispy
  5. Enjoy!
